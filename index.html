Research Interest

My research interest is machine learning, temporal convolutional networks, graph nerural network, computer vision approaches and their application to driver identification, driving profiling, driver intent prediction, scene understanding and motion prediction for intelligent transportation systems and autonomous vehicles. I am currently working on Driving Behavior Analysis (DBA); more specifically, my research now is focused on extracting high quality and efficient driver embeddings from driving time series using scalable representation learning; and enabling efficient graph-based spatio temporal convolutions to model the interactive behavioral patterns of multiple agents within a driving scene, at unsignalized intersections in particular.
